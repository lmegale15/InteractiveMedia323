Untitled

Death       is       not       the      end



Nor           the           final           word



Death           is           a           separation




Temporarily             unheard



Death               has               no               power



To             keep             apart



A           love          forged           so           deeply



In         a         person's         heart




Death      creates        a        veil



And      shields      us      from      view



But    death    can   do    nothing



To disconnect me from you



~Tanya Lord
